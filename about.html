about us

Bonvas Tours Limited is a technology-driven and sustainable tour operating company located in Ghana, Africa. We offer comprehensive travel and tour services to groups, individuals, and institutions travelling to and from Ghana and other countries in the sub-region of Africa. Established in 2018 by private individuals with extensive experience in hospitality and tourism, Bonvas Tours aims to promote tourism in Ghana among locals, the diaspora, and tourists alike.

Bonvas Tours Limited is located at KNUST Commercial Area inside Opoku Ware II Museum in Kumasi and East Legon Otinshie Accra, Ghana - West Africa. Our offices are open from 0900hrs to 1700hrs, with a 24/7 customer center number. Bonvas Tours operates with an autonomous structure while maintaining a centralized administration to ensure high service quality and efficient delivery. The company has six (6) departments with nine (9) sections and teams. Each department has a specific mandate to fulfil. The departments include the administrative department, sales and marketing department, accounts and finance department, outbound department, IT & Innovation department, and Operations department. Bonvas Tours Limited provides high-quality travel and tourism services to individuals, groups, and institutions with an emphasis on customer satisfaction and building long-term relationships.

Swiftnano

Swiftnano was a transportation service called Bonvas Campus Transport Services, which offered bus rentals and organized bus services for various universities and students. Swiftnano is an online bus booking system that allows its users to check for the availability of buses scheduled for departure in the system, select their preferred bus and seat, confirm the booking, and make payments all online without leaving their comfort zone. The system is designed to solve the everyday frustrations of the travelling population and provide the user with 360-degree assistance when it comes to travelling from one destination to another. Swiftnano; Accessible, Affordable, and Reliable.
