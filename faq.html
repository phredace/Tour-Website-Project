FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

To book a tour with Bonvas Tours, please visit our website and fill out the online booking form. You will need to provide your contact information, the tour you would like to book, and the dates of your travel. Once you have submitted the form, one of our travel agents will contact you to confirm your booking.

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the tour package and the time of cancellation. We offer a flexible cancellation policy. If you cancel your booking more than 45 days before the tour, you will receive a full refund. If you cancel between 16 and 30 days before the tour, you will receive a 60% refund. If you cancel less than 15 days before the tour, you will not receive a refund.

You can cancel your booking by emailing info@bonvastours.com or calling us at +233 261 671 686.

We understand that sometimes things happen that are beyond our control. If you have to cancel your booking for a reason beyond your control, we may be able to offer you a partial refund. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation.

The cancellation policy is available on our website and in our terms and conditions. You can also find a copy of the cancellation policy by contacting us.

Flight inclusions vary for each tour package. Some packages may include flights, while others may only cover land arrangements. Please refer to the package details or contact our team for specific information regarding flights and inclusions.

Travel visas and vaccinations are generally not included in our tour packages. It is the responsibility of each traveler to ensure they have the necessary visas and vaccinations required for their destination. We recommend checking with the relevant embassy or consulate and consulting with your healthcare professional for up-to-date information. Our Team will be on standby to assist you through the process.

Yes, we offer the flexibility to customize tour packages to suit your preferences. Whether it's adjusting the itinerary, adding specific activities, or choosing accommodations, our team will work with you to create a personalized experience. Contact our customer service team to discuss your customization requests.

The group size varies depending on the tour package. Some tours may have a maximum group size, while others may be more intimate with smaller groups. Details regarding group size can be found in the tour package description. If you have specific requirements or preferences, please let us know, and we will assist you accordingly. However, we generally offer tours in three group sizes: small, medium, and large. Small groups (4-15 people) are ideal for travelers who want a more intimate and personalized experience. Medium groups (16-30 people) offer a good balance of intimacy and affordability. Large groups (31+ people) are a good option for travelers who want to be part of a larger group and experience the camaraderie of traveling with a group of people.

We highly recommend purchasing travel insurance to protect your investment and provide coverage for unforeseen circumstances. While we do not provide travel insurance directly, our team can assist you in finding suitable insurance options based on your needs.

We accept bank transfer & mobile money

Transportation details are specified in each tour package. Depending on the itinerary, transportation may include private vehicles, shared transfers, or public transportation. Our packages are designed to ensure comfortable and convenient transportation throughout your journey.

We strive to accommodate special dietary requirements whenever possible. Please inform us of any specific dietary restrictions or allergies during the booking process, and we will do our best to make suitable arrangements. However, please note that certain destinations or remote areas may have limited options for specific dietary needs. Bonvas Tours can accommodate a variety of special needs, including but not limited to: Mobility limitations: We can provide wheelchairs and other mobility aids, as well as accessible transportation. Hearing impairments: We can provide sign language interpreters and other accommodations for travelers with hearing impairments. Dietary restrictions: We can accommodate a variety of dietary restrictions, including allergies, intolerances, and religious restrictions.

Our customer service team is available to assist you 24/7. You can reach us by phone +233 261 671 686, or email info@bonvastours.com, visit the Contact Us page for the relevant contact information. We are here to answer your questions and provide the support you need.

The age restrictions for our tours vary depending on the tour package. However, we generally recommend that travelers be at least 12 years old to participate in our tours. Some tours may have higher age restrictions, so please be sure to check the specific tour package description for more information

Bonvas Tours takes the safety and security of our travelers very seriously. We have a comprehensive safety and security program in place that includes: Trained and experienced tour leaders Regular safety checks of our vehicles and equipment Strict adherence to all local safety regulations A 24/7 emergency assistance service
